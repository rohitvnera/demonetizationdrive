About

Objective

Since November 8th, 2016 evening, when honourable PM of India Sri Narendra Modi announced the demonetization scheme for old Rs. 500 and Rs. 1000 bills, our way of handling cash has changed dramatically. The dispersal of the new replacement currency of Rs 2000 and new Rs. 500 bills into the system, will take sometime.With bulk of liquid cash in circulation being in “now demonetized” Rs. 500 and Rs. 1000 denominations, there is a sudden drop in cash liquidity, for small day to day transactions.

Long term positive impacts of this scheme are immense, but in short term we as a common citizen are facing some difficulties.

Authorities in conjunction with banking system, are trying their best to alleviate the hardships being faced by the common citizen. Banks are working overtime and on weekends, to meet the exchange and withdrawal needs of the

One thing though - not all ATMs get emptied at the same time. Basically at any given time, if not your usual ATM, then some other ATM in your neighbourhood might be dispensing cash, only if you knew which one.

indiafindbank.in is an attempt to provide you with this information, so that you can reach the correct ATM spot and withdraw cash, a bit more easily.

What you get

A Google Map driven, live map of ATMs and bank branches in your vicinity, tagged with information

  • whether thery are dispensing cash or not
  • if not then by when will they start dispensing cash.

The information will be kept as live as possible, based on frequent inputs from various banks directly, and contributions from users directly.

How can you help

In the initial phase of the operation of this system, it will rely on inputs from general user of the system, to get the cash status updates of ATMs. So contributions from individual users will be of great value to the system. Subsequently, the inputs can be arranged straight from bank officials, adding to the quality of the status data. So as a user of this system, you can be of help to others by updating the system as an when you get time and any new information, like this

It is a trust based system from well meaning citizens for each other, so try to be as accurate and timely in your updates as possible, in return you will get the accurate and timely updates from others.
